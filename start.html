Frequently Asked Questions

What types of cargo do you transport? We handle parcels, pallets, oversized goods, commercial shipments, and temperature-sensitive cargo across Denmark.
Do you offer tracking? Yes, all shipments include real-time tracking so you can monitor your delivery at every stage of its journey.
How fast is delivery? Delivery speed depends on distance and cargo type, but we prioritize fast, optimized routes to reduce waiting time.
Can I schedule regular shipments? Absolutely. We offer scheduled routes and recurring transport plans for businesses needing continuous logistics support.
Do you operate internationally? Our primary focus is Denmark, but we also support cross-border transport and can arrange international deliveries upon request.
How do I get a price estimate? Contact our team for a personalized quote based on distance, cargo weight, and delivery preferences.
Do you offer express delivery? Yes, express and same-day delivery options are available for urgent shipments.
Can I change my delivery details? Yes, simply reach out to us, and we’ll make adjustments wherever possible.
