2. Services

FastFlow Cargo provides domestic and international freight transport, express delivery, warehousing, and related logistics services within Denmark and Europe.

3. Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate shipment details and documentation.
  • Ensure cargo is packaged securely and complies with legal standards.
  • Inform us of hazardous, fragile, or special-handling items.

Our liability for cargo damage or loss follows Danish transport legislation and international conventions applicable to road freight.
