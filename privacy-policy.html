Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

FastFlow Cargo (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our logistics and freight services in Denmark and Europe.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, phone number, email address.
  • Business information: company name, VAT number, billing details.
  • Shipment data: cargo type, route information, pickup and delivery addresses.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and manage freight and transport services.
  • Communicate regarding orders, updates, and support.
  • Improve route planning and operational efficiency.
  • Ensure safety, compliance, and fraud prevention.

4. Data Sharing

Your information may be shared with:

  • Transport partners and subcontracted carriers.
  • Customs offices and regulatory authorities (if required).
  • Payment service providers.
  • IT and hosting providers located in the EU.

5. Data Protection & Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse according to Danish and EU regulations (GDPR).

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your data.
  • Right to correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to request deletion of data.
  • Right to restrict or object to data processing.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at: info@fastflowcargo.dk
